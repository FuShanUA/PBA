Palantir for the US Army

The Software Advantage for Multi-Domain Operations.

At Palantir, we believe that software is the critical enabler of modern mission systems.

Since 2008, we’ve proudly partnered with the US Army, embedding alongside users to design and deploy modern mission essential software solutions.

Today, Palantir's solutions are deployed across nearly every Army mission area, ensuring data is accessible across all echelons for fast, agile decision-making that allows the warfighter to out-think and out-pace the adversary.

Powering Software Advantage Across Environments

Our solutions operate as the connective tissue between the Army’s personnel, data, and resources by enabling the surfacing of critical information to the right decision-makers at the right time.

  • Soldier
  • Ground
  • Airborne
  • Edge
  • Enterprise

Soldier

Remarkable advances in wearable sensor and mobile technologies have proven their potential to revolutionize the battlefield. However, the data produced by these accessories is immense, and risks overwhelming both the individual Soldiers who leverage them and the battlefield decision-makers who need to manage and interpret them.

Palantir offers solutions to harness the power of these hardware solutions, reduce system complexity, and provide improved human-machine interfaces to assist Soldiers in the field and Commanders at the FOB. From situation awareness powered by visual augmentation, to sensor optimization for improved targeting and fires capability, Palantir solutions integrate secure capabilities to help reduce cognitive burden, protect, and connect the warfighter.

Ground

Intelligent ground systems are at the heart of the Army’s realization of a multi-domain operations concept. To empower commanders to make sense of an increasingly dynamic and complex battlefield, these ground systems are key to understanding and managing the battlefield.

At Palantir, we are investing in bringing software to the core of these critical command and control nodes, connecting them to assets across domains – from Space, High Altitude, Aerial, and Terrestrial layers – for improved targeting and situational awareness and understanding.

  • Learn more about how Palantir is working to build the future of ground systems in Multi-Domain Operations.

Airborne

New aviation modernization efforts extend the reach of Army intelligence, manpower, and equipment to dynamically deter the threat at extended range. At Palantir, we deploy AI/ML-enabled solutions onto airborne platforms so that users can see farther, generate insights faster, and react at the speed of relevance.

Our technologies are helping to advance aviation capabilities for the US and its allies - from fielding and managing on-platform software solutions for target recognition and threat characterization, to advancing distributed C2 through teaming and collaboration across platforms. 

Edge

As more sensors are deployed to the farthest reaches of the battlefield, networks must adapt to physical and environmental challenges. Palantir Edge AI ensures users maintain uninterrupted access to data at the tactical edge.

Palantir's AI orchestration and sensor fusion engine are designed to scale and operate in austere environments. Designed for situations where time and efficiency matter, it operates in low-bandwidth, low-power conditions — including on drones, aircraft, ships, robots, buildings, and satellites. 

  • Learn more about how Palantir is empowering the edge. 

Enterprise

For the US Army to meet its digital transformation goals, authoritative data from across the enterprise must be integrated, managed, and made accessible.

The Palantir platform was built to integrate and transform large scale data for robust analytics and to surface actionable operational insights. With continuous feedback loops between data, analytics, and scenario planning, the Palantir platform helps power decisions that create compounding value. 

  • Learn how Army Vantage, powered by Palantir, unifies the Army’s fractured data landscape to accelerate and enhance decision-making across the force.

  • Discover how the Army Intelligence Data Platform, an all-source intelligence solution developed in close collaboration with Army Military Intelligence Brigades, gives warfighters a critical edge.
