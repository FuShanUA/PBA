Interoperable to the core

Palantir platforms are interoperable across the full stack, bringing your institution's data engineering, data science, and operations onto one platform. Your data becomes flexibly available to power the entire enterprise, whether you’re chaining models, building applications, or making decisions in the field.

Open Technology Platforms

Palantir is an open, interoperable platform designed to maximize the value you can derive from your data. By using open data formats and providing several options to export any data or analysis that exists in the system, Palantir ensures your unfettered ownership of your data.

Palantir also provides open interfaces for customization and extension. Palantir’s open APIs can be used to tailor the platform to meet a variety of needs. As an open system that uses open data formats, Palantir is interoperable and extensible to accommodate an evolving technology landscape.

Don't replace. Orchestrate.

Organizations often find themselves spending as much time managing relationships between systems as they do building or using the systems themselves.

Palantir software integrates and orchestrates these data systems. Data from your integrated systems is available directly for our out-of-the-box applications, APIs, and application development frameworks. Developers can use our platform to quickly and easily build applications that leverage the relevant data in their source systems, without the added burden of systems integration — and with or without new lines of code.

Often, our customers already have existing technologies they rely on to operate. However, these tools were built to satisfy and maintain daily operations — not to create competitive advantage. Rather than replace these tools, our software serves as a substrate, binding an enterprise’s IT landscape together. By orchestrating and augmenting the operations of the existing technologies, our software maximizes their value.
