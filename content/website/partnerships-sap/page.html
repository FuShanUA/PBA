Palantir + SAP

Accelerating the path to cloud for mission-critical systems and unlocking the new era of AI capabilities

Palantir and SAP are embarking on a joint engineering effort to accelerate, secure, and modernize mission-critical operations for customers – from foundational SAP ERP Central Component (SAP ECC) functions to advanced, frontline AI capabilities.

Over the past few decades, institutions have invested deeply in SAP ECC capabilities that underpin critical functions – from finance and logistics to procurement and asset management. Many of these customers have worked closely with Palantir to leverage their foundational business data to deliver enterprise automation powered by the Palantir Ontology and delivered through Foundry and AIP.

The AI revolution requires customers to modernize their traditional on-prem systems and transition into a cloud-native operation. This not only enables more efficient, secure delivery of existing workflows, but also accelerates the delivery of AI-powered outcomes.

Existing migration approaches are costly, lengthy, and introduce meaningful interruptions to critical operations. Customers require AI advantage now.

The SAP and Palantir partnership focuses on rapidly delivering synergy across our proven strengths:

  • The accelerated adoption of defense-grade Cloud ERP through SAP National Security Services (SAP NS2), supporting secure, compliant mission execution at global scale.

  • Delivery of enterprise-wide autonomous workflows via Palantir Foundry and AIP, and powered by Ontology

Technical Delivery

Optimize existing integrations via dedicated joint development

Building on a longstanding and battle-tested history of connecting to SAP systems through certified connectors such as HyperAuto, this partnership deepens joint efforts to ensure secure, bidirectional data access through modern interfaces. SAP and Palantir recognize the value these integrations deliver and the performance demands they carry. We will protect existing investments while unlocking new value – powered by secure, real-time data flow across both platforms. Our teams are committed to providing a supported, non-disruptive path for joint customers, ensuring HyperAuto remains the primary connector throughout the transition from SAP ECC to SAP Cloud ERP Private, including those leveraging the RISE with SAP journey.

Accelerate modernizations efforts via dedicated migration products and customer engagement

The RISE with SAP journey offers a guided, low-disruption path to the cloud – offering flexibility, faster deployment, and long-term cost efficiency, all while meeting strict security and compliance standards. By combining SAP’s enterprise applications, data, and embedded AI with Palantir’s AI-driven automation and analytics, the partnership aims to shorten timelines, target high-value datasets, and scale integrations across existing and new deployments.

Compound the value AI investments across both platforms in cloud-native environments

SAP and Palantir are collaborating to amplify AI capabilities across cloud-native environments. By connecting SAP Business Data Cloud’s unified, context-rich data environment—which leverages SAP’s zero-copy architecture and collaborative framework—with Palantir’s powerful Ontology and AIP, customers benefit from the complementary strengths of both platforms. This bidirectional integration will enhance the value customers derive from SAP’s Business AI and embedded AI scenarios, as well as from Palantir’s advanced autonomous operations. The result is a multiplier effect that transforms enterprise data into actionable, real-time intelligence – bridging enterprise insights with tactical operations without compromising security or duplicating data.

Looking ahead

Given the criticality of the workflows built across our platforms, Palantir and SAP are dedicated to technical and strategic integrations that maximize outcomes and minimize disruptions.
