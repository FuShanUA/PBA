AIP for EHS Risk Management

AIP for Environment, Health and Safety (EHS) Risk Management empowers organizations to enhance safety performance by analyzing, identifying, and predicting risks, enabling proactive hazard mitigation and fostering a culture of safety across all levels of the workforce.

AIP for EHS Risk Management transforms risk management in industrial environments by integrating Environmental, Health, and Safety (EHS) data—including safety reports, reportable incidents, and observational data from disparate sources. Coupled with asset information from ERP and CMMS systems, it provides operators with a comprehensive understanding of the risk landscape across their sites. Utilizing smart models, AIP identifies and helps users to predict the likelihood of reportable incidents in specific sites, assets, or processes, and recommends mitigation strategies, including proactive stakeholder communication, to prevent potential hazards. Key Features:

  • Integration of EHS Data: AIP for EHS Risk Management amalgamates both structured site data and unstructured information such as reportable incidents and safety reports, creating a digital twin of operational safety.

  • Comprehensive Mission Control View: Offers a holistic view of EHS performance by combining risk assessments, reportable incidents, and operations & maintenance (O&M) processes, enabling users to pinpoint critical risk areas efficiently.

  • Smart Prediction Models: These models assist users in evaluating operations and pinpointing sites with the highest risk of reportable incidents, enabling them to take targeted preventive measures and make informed decisions.

  • Proactive Alerting: Automatically alerts stakeholders of high-risk sites, enhancing communication and enabling swift action to mitigate identified risks.
