Foundry in Action

  • Revenue Growth Management

Partner
A global beverage manufacturer
Challenge

The customer needed an integrated, end-to-end sales and revenue management system to consolidate hundreds of regionally specific workloads that managed over 1M SKUs. Annual price increase processes took weeks to complete.

Solution

Clustering methodology created a more standardized and contextually informed annual price increase process across 1M+ products. Localized elasticity models show revenue trade-offs between opposing priorities such as increasing prices and increasing volume.

Impact

↳ $5.4M savings from product and pricing strategy

↳ 2.2X increase in number of SKU prices optimized

↳ $2.6M savings from identifying where wholesalers are misaligned with pricing strategy

Partner
A Fortune 100 CPG
Challenge

The customer needed to unlock data across 7+ ERP systems to understand how to improve production and optimize raw materials purchases. Data preparation and analysis required a costly manual process that took weeks to complete.

Solution

Within days, Foundry integrated 7+ ERP data sources to produce a “digital twin” of the supply chain using Foundry’s Ontology. With the Ontology, analysts built a granular COGS and profitability model to apply on the SKU level. Purchasing teams use an out-of-the-box Bill of Material workflow to optimize COGS and production.

Impact

↳ $100M est. annual savings

↳ $10s of millions est. savings in COGS

↳ 5 days to ingest 7+ ERP sources into the Ontology

Partner
A large CPG manufacturer
Challenge

Orders can undergo many changes before being shipped, including adjustments to weight and size, which can result in underutilized trucks. The customer had no system in place to optimize and re-optimize load utilization throughout the order management process.

Solution

Foundry’s Load Consolidation Module constantly analyzes the impact of incoming changes and re-optimizes the trucking plan across a range of constraints and variables. It suggests loads to consolidate, over-allocated orders to cut, etc., in order to ensure an optimal execution plan.

Impact

↳ $6M+ in annualized savings using the tool

↳ 200+ trucks removed each month due to optimization & consolidation
