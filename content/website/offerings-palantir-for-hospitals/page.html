Overview

As the largest hospital network in New York City, Mount Sinai Health System turned to Palantir to build solutions that would enable them to make data-driven decisions across multiple workflows, and drive better financial and patient outcomes.

Examples of Our Work Together

Partner
Tampa General Hospital
Overview

Through partnering with Palantir’s team of forward-deployed engineers, Tampa General Hospital (TGH) is able to centralize their wealth of data into a single source of truth, which they leverage to help reduce length of hospital stays for patients and perform life-saving work.

Examples of Our Work Together

Intelligent patient and staffing allocation → TGH leverages Palantir to power predictive analytics in their post-anesthesia care unit to ensure accurate daily allocation of their 1100 beds across 1200 – 1300 patients and greatly reduce the manual effort previously required to perform this task.

Clinical Staffing and EVS Staffing → Real-time visualization of actual vs target staff-to-patient ratios to inform day-of float and long-term staffing estimated over $1M saved in labor expenses, 57% reduction in EVS agency spend, and 25% EVS cleaning turnaround time improvement.

Partner
Cleveland Clinic
Overview

Cleveland Clinic leverages Palantir to build a platform for care delivery across all avenues of hospital operations. This includes streamlining patient bed assignment and discharge management to reduce wait times and length of stay, adjusting staff allocation to facilitate optimal care, and honing utilization of critical resources (e.g., operating room block times).

Examples of Our Work Together

Capacity Management → Combining real-time and predicted demand for patient beds with staffing insights enables earlier and enhanced visibility into bed demand across the hospital, which ultimately allows for increased admission of transfer patients, and a decrease in ED and PACU hold times.

Proactive Staffing: Putting both patient care and nurse satisfaction at the center, these new tools give nursing leaders the insights needed to balance schedules and optimize resources by proactively recommending day-of solutions, and surfacing downstream impacts.
