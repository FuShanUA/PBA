AIP for Asset Maintenance Scheduling

AIP for Asset Maintenance Scheduling streamlines the planning and execution of asset maintenance, combining automated scheduling with a suite of management tools to optimize wrench time and enhance the health of site assets.

Planner schedulers are tasked with coordinating technician workloads, work orders, permits, and asset maintenance programs to maximize uptime and extend the life of essential water treatment assets. At the core of this balancing act is Asset Maintenance Scheduling. AIP utilizes integrated data processed through optimization algorithms that blend Jacobs-defined best practices with tailored site-specific rules, delivering optimal weekly work order schedules. This sophisticated system ensures efficient maintenance operations, improved asset health, and enhanced site productivity. Key Features:

  • Automated Scheduling: Employs Jacobs best practices and customizable constraints within the scheduling algorithm to help planner schedulers optimize work order prioritization, technician expertise, and asset proximity, maximizing valuable wrench time and improving overall site health.

  • Interactive Scheduling UI: Provides planner schedulers with an interactive interface to fine-tune and adjust schedules before publication, accommodating last-minute changes and unexpected events.

  • CMMS Writeback: Seamlessly integrates with the Technician Portal by writing back finalized schedules, ensuring that work orders are readily accessible to technicians, thus facilitating efficient task execution.

AIP for Asset Maintenance Scheduling is an essential tool for ensuring that maintenance activities are performed efficiently and effectively, keeping critical infrastructure in peak condition and supporting the operational goals of water treatment facilities.
