AIP for Maintenance Assistance

Drive maintenance best practices by giving technicians instructions tailored to the work at hand. AIP brings together work order details, asset history, and maintenance manuals to promote safe, efficient, and reliable maintenance work.

AIP for Maintenance Assistance brings together CMMS, ERP, and Maintenance Manuals to give technicians the full context needed to more efficiently and safely complete their maintenance work. AIP considers the specific work required, the asset's operational history, and best practices from technical manuals to deliver a unified knowledge base. Technicians can interactively query AIP for work guidance, safety procedures, historical repairs, and more, with each response linked to its source documentation for verification and in-depth review — providing peace of mind and the ability to dig deeper into the source material. Key Features:

  • Semantic Search: Integrates and searches through various documents, labor reports, and past inquiries to generate informative responses tailored to the particular asset data.

  • Interactive Engagement with AIP: Maintains a contextual dialogue history, allowing users to delve further into AIP's responses and seek additional clarification as needed.

  • Document Viewer: Offers a built-in viewer to cross-reference AIP's responses with the source material, enhancing trust and enabling technicians to consult the original documentation.

  • Document Uploader: Provides a straightforward interface for uploading new maintenance manuals, expanding the semantic search database and keeping the system's knowledge up-to-date.

AIP for Maintenance Assistance is a vital tool for maintenance technicians, delivering instant access to essential information and guidance, thereby improving the quality and safety of maintenance activities across industries.
