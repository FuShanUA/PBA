Get Certified

Accelerate and Validate Foundry Expertise

The Palantir Foundry Certification Program provides an opportunity for Foundry users to highlight their knowledge, skills, and abilities. Start exploring Foundry Certifications today. Existing Foundry customers and third-party partners can gain access by reaching out to their point-of-contact at Palantir.

Exam Tracks

Certifications by Role

Foundry Certification exams are available in two role-based tracks detailed below.

App Developer Certification: Demonstrate your ability to design and create an ontology in Foundry and build operational applications on top of it.

Data Engineering Certification: Demonstrate your ability to use Foundry to develop, maintain, and validate data pipelines.

Prep & Support

Exam Guides and More

Candidates for the Foundry Certification exams are individuals who have experience using Foundry to deliver relevant outcomes in their desired exam track. Exam guides will be provided to all Certification candidates prior to sitting for the exam. These guides break down the topics covered by each assessment and provide links to relevant documentation and tutorials to help candidates prepare.

Use Case Approach

Expert Support for Ambitious Use Cases

Use Case Boost is a structured program that provides Palantir expert support to your team’s use case development and execution so they can best achieve their targeted outcomes.

It’s designed to accelerate use case implementation and increase the likelihood of successful productization through access to our cross-industry experience, use case best practices, and Palantir subject matter experts.

Use Case Methodology

Tailored Training to Meet Your Use Case Need

Delivery Method

Use Case Boost is a tailored program that offers scoping sessions, solution design workshops, and recurring steering meetings.

Duration

Use Case Boost is a 6-week engagement to accelerate projects of any scope while building momentum and Foundry skills that carry over after the Use Case Boost concludes.

Palantir Experts

Use Case Boosts are delivered by Palantir experts. As appropriate, we bring in Palantir Foundry subject matter experts to deep dive into a particular product or a challenging workflow.
