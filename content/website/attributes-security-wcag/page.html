Website Content Accessibility Guidelines and Section 508 Compliance

Palantir software solutions have been approved to operate in environments where WCAG and U.S. Section 508 accessibility standards are required.

The Foundry configuration of the Palantir Platform can be configured to substantially conform with WCAG 2.1 guidelines. Foundry has been approved to operate in environments where WCAG and Section 508 accessibility standards are required, including many federal agency customers. Foundry supports responsive web design, can work in multiple web browsers, and can be configured to support accessibility standards for navigation, operational controls, readability, help, and documentation.

Our Product Design and Implementation team assigned to the project is dedicated to configuring accessible software for a diverse population of users and will work with customers to address specific requirements or concerns. Further, the teams coordinate throughout the year with the Palantir compliance team to maintain accessibility standards and develop strategies to continue product accessibility in new product offerings.
