Palantir & NCMEC

Fighting Child Exploitation with Big Data

Play Video

Last year alone, the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) received 16.9 million reports of images and videos depicting child sexual abuse. With a team of just 30 analysts sorting through as many as 60,000 reports a day, timely triage is a challenge — and when it comes to rescuing victims, every second counts.

Since 2010, Palantir has partnered with NCMEC, providing our data integration software in support of its mission to help find missing children, reduce child sexual exploitation, and prevent future victimization. A short film by Freethink Media takes viewers on the front lines with NCMEC analysts and law enforcement personnel, who share how Palantir software aids in their efforts to identify and rescue child victims.

To learn more about the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children, visit https://www.missingkids.org.
