Performant Software is No Longer a Luxury.

The dominant nation-states and companies that define our way of life will be the ones that get software and use of data right. Getting this right is a requirement to preserve our way of life and enable society to thrive beyond a few dominant organizations.

At Palantir, we recognize our customers' existential need to use software to generate competitive advantage or, alpha. We deliver the outcomes that matter to our customers by always aligning ourselves with their success, and investing deeply to deliver it. This approach is at odds with commodity SaaS that has come to dominate the market. The market incentivizes solutions that provide incremental efficiency at the lowest common denominator — but zero competitive advantage to the end customer.

Wall Street often criticizes our unique approach. Our customers do not. Our customers see data not as exhaust, but as the very fuel that powers their mission. They recognize that most software — whose ambition is the modern day equivalent of the Dewey Decimal System — is insufficient for their future. They understand that analytic silos, automation of inefficient decisions, and calcified operating systems are not enough to dominate their space in 2025. They require connected ecosystems that constantly talk, improve, and compound upon one another.

For these enterprises, Palantir builds the foundational software of tomorrow, and we deliver it today. We've advanced beyond incremental efficiencies to bring intelligence to the core of everything that matters most to your enterprise.
