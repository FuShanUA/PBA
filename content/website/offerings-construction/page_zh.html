The Construction Ontology

The Construction Ontology integrates all core functions—from pre-construction to procurement, job site execution, and project closeout—through a unified semantic layer.

At its core, the Ontology standardizes how departments interact with shared business objects like projects, activities, equipment, and subcontractors, ensuring consistent definitions and real-time synchronization across the enterprise.

Built from a field-first perspective, the Ontology connects what happens on the job site to the systems that support it in a low/no code environment that is accessible to every user, regardless of technical background.

Foundry bridges the gap between foundational platforms (ERP, project management, Building Information Modeling (BIM), accounting, equipment management) and critical use cases. It enables AI-powered decisions that predict scheduling delays before they hit, accelerate contractor and subcontractor alignment, and synthesize project delivery with safety, budget, and schedule goals, ultimately reducing risk across execution—all while preserving existing technology investments.
