AIP for Sustainable Solutions

AIP for Sustainable Solutions leverages LLM-powered insights to guide and track environmental, social, and economic impact of projects, driving adherence to client and company commitments while measuring performance aligned to the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

AIP for Sustainable Solutions is a cutting-edge application designed to embed sustainability into the heart of project planning and execution. By fusing project data with Jacobs' extensive sustainability expertise and established best practices, the system can suggest project objectives that promote positive sustainable impact and align performance to the UN Sustainable Development Goals as well as other ESG reporting frameworks. Recommended performance indicators are then meticulously tracked throughout the project's delivery lifecycle, ensuring that sustainability remains a core focus from inception to completion. Key Features:

  • LLM-Powered Sustainability Engine: Harnesses best practices and insights from Jacobs subject matter experts to pinpoint the most pertinent sustainability goals for your projects while tracking environmental, social, and economic impact. Learns from historical project performance to refine and enhance recommendations.

  • Interactive Goal Refinement: Engage with AIP for Sustainable Solutions through a conversational interface, enabling users to customize sustainability objectives to best suit their specific project demands.

  • Seamless Project Management Integration: AIP for Sustainable Solutions seamlessly incorporates into existing project management platforms, fortified with robust security measures to safeguard sensitive project data.

AIP for Sustainable Solutions is an essential tool for any organization striving to meet ambitious sustainability benchmarks, providing a strategic advantage in achieving project outcomes that drive positive impact.
