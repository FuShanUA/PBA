Publications

Palantir is an artist colony, with opinions and ideas to share with the world.

Palantirians manage and maintain many platforms for us to express our views on technology, politics, philosophy, history, and culture - and help non-Palantirians do the same. Explore these digital publications below.

  • The Republic

    The Republic is managed by the Palantir Foundation, a nonprofit founded by Palantir in 2023. It publishes innovative articles and book reviews at the intersection of technology policy, national security, and international relations. The Republic publishes both Palantirians and other guest writers.

  • The Palantir Blog

    The Palantir Blog was launched in 2016 as the first outlet for Palantirians to publish long-form written essays. It helps the company communicate important information about our business, our products, our internal culture, and our approach to Privacy and Civil Liberties with the world.

  • First Breakfast

    First Breakfast is a newsletter on the history of American defense procurement and how we can mobilize our industrial base to supercharge the defense tech ecosystem. It is written by Shyam Sankar (Chief Technology Officer of Palantir), Madeline Zimmerman, Greg Little, Blake Seitz, other Palantir employees, and guest authors.

  • Artist's Colony

    Artist’s Colony is a publication from Palantir’s Public Policy and Government Affairs team. We write about the issues at the center of data, AI, and software policy — AI governance, digital sovereignty, privacy, security, and the practical realities of deploying AI in high-stakes environments.

  • Working Intelligence

    Working Intelligence was launched in 2025 to explore how the deployment of artificial intelligence is augmenting the American worker. It publishes both a newsletter and a podcast.
