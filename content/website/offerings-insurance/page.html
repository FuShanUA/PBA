Winning with AI Automation in Insurance

AI automation delivering speed and accuracy to critical business processes across insurance and beyond.

In a world that is becoming increasingly fast-paced with growing risk and uncertainty, it’s crucial for the insurance industry to make timely, accurate decisions contextualized from high volumes of unstructured data across siloed sources, while staying compliant with ever-evolving and stringent regulatory requirements.

All across the insurance value chain, partners are relying on Palantir software to power critical decisions — leveraging our state-of-the-art AI and automation capabilities to more accurately underwrite risk, process claims, and unlock efficiencies in day to day operations.

AI Underwriting Automation

Data Extraction

AIP extracts relevant data attributes from thousands of structured and unstructured sources, including PDFs, emails, Excel spreadsheets, audio calls, and more.

Augmented Decision-making

Extracted submissions data is compared against numerous sources of information including historical performance data, similar cases, predefined underwriting guidelines set by your organization, etc. to inform accurate and compliant underwriting decisions.

Actuarial Pricing and Reserving Integration

AIP integrates bi-directionally with existing actuarial models, pricing raters, and reserving engines to provide proposed premium and reserve amounts.

Auto-generated Responses

AIP enables underwriters to generate suggested responses to brokers and agents based on a combination of automated flags generated and human-in-the-loop interventions.

Auditability

All actions taken by the user and by AI Agent are written back and recorded in AIP, ensuring traceability into the decision lineage.

AI Smart Claims

Claims FNOL Ingestion

All sources of structured and unstructured information as part of the first notification of loss (FNOL) are centralized and integrated together - emails, call recordings, images, etc.

Multimodal-AI Automated Review

AIP leverages multimodal AI to automatically assess incoming claims against pre-defined and customizable coverage conditions, ensuring a rapid and accurate determination of coverage eligibility.

Smart Claims Assessment Routing

For each incoming claim that needs assessment, a dynamic routing instruction matches each claim with the "best" claims assessment human or channel to minimise cost of review and complexity minimization.

Rapid Automated Payouts

AI Agents handle the claims that pass all appropriate coverage checks and perform direct integration with payout systems for immediate payout — encouraging customer satisfaction and retention.

AI Claims Analytics

Surfacing trends across cohorts of the business to identify growing unexpected losses, perform actuals-vs-expected experience studies, and continuously update reserving and IBNR estimations. Remove bottlenecks in the claims process with state-reviews.

Ethical AI Guardrails

AIP puts guardrails in place to assure the AI is not permitted to prevent the payout of a claim without satisfying human-enforced rules and human confirmation of the correctness of its determinations.

Real-time AI Customer Intelligence

AI-Powered Customer 360

Integrate structured and unstructured data from internal, external, and open sources to create a holistic customer record, augmented with AI and model driven data enrichments.

Next Best Action / Next Best Product

Provide AI recommendations for marketing campaigns, propensity-to-purchase, propensity-to-bind, cross-sell, and up-sell across the customer base.

Real-time audio-to-text transcription

AIP transcribes and intelligently extracts information from live phone calls, with support for 50+ languages for use during customer interactions.

AI-Powered Live Suggestions

During live interactions, AIP automatically suggests follow-up questions for claim handlers and call center employees to aid in accurate information capture and facilitate a first-class customer experience.

Call Summary & Next Best Action

AIP generates a summary of the phone call in a comprehensive, easy to read format, and suggests next best actions for the claims handler to take based on data from the interaction.

Partner
Large European Insurer
Overview

A large European insurer needed a way to process the millions of incoming claims it received annually. However, the extensive variance in document contents and formats made full automation of evaluation steps impossible without AI.

Examples of our Work Together

Instant claim ingestion via API call: Incoming unstructured claims are ontologized in AIP within seconds after being received by the client’s claim server.

AI-driven structuring of claims: 20+ required claim properties are automatically extracted and interpreted using AI models.

Writeback to operational systems: Once all required details have been successfully extracted, the now-structured claims are written to the client’s claims handling system and archive databases.

Computer vision and AI-driven structuring of claims: A computer vision model is used to store all ingested claims in a machine-readable format. An LLM then extracts & interprets the required details.

Autonomous coverage evaluation: Claims are automatically evaluated using a set of insurance policy rules. These rules are maintained by policy experts in AIP using a low-code UI.

Automatic payout execution: The evaluation results are sent to the client’s invoice payout system, which then automatically performs the determined deductions and payouts to customers.
