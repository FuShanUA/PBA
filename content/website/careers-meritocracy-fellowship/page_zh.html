We Believe

You don't have to wait until 2030 to do real things.

The college industrial complex wants you on their timeline: four years of prerequisites, debt, and indoctrination. While they debate DEI vs. SATs, we're focused on one thing: building.

We launched the Meritocracy Fellowship to cultivate exceptional talent, increasingly overlooked, regardless of background.

We believe in the power and agency of young Americans to shape our hardest problems. Not someday. Now.

We believe those with the highest aptitude deserve challenges, not a set curriculum; agency, not merely a credential; and responsibility, not busy work.

We believe meritocracy is the operating system for execution.

The Fellowship

To defend the West effectively, we first must wrestle with it.

What is the West? Is it worth defending? In what ways is America exceptional within this broader context?

At Palantir, Fellows begin their journey by exploring these fundamental questions through curated readings and seminars with leading experts. This initial period is designed to immerse Fellows in rigorous debate and open exchange of ideas—laying the intellectual foundation before they join forward-deployed engineering or internal software teams.

In our inaugural cohort, Fellows engaged with world-class historians, philosophers, scientists, writers, and technologists, including Gideon Rose, Edward Wittenstein, Bob McGrew, Sara Seager, Reihan Salaam, Hal Brands, Shilo Brooks, Larry Arn, Lawrence Wright, Adam Klein, Brendan McCord, Lt. General (ret.) George Smith, Bob Langer, Itai Yanai, among others. They also learned from Palantirians such as Dr. Alex Karp, Shyam Sankar, Ted Mabrey, Akshay Kirshnaswamy, Courtney Bowman, Nicholas Zamiska, Samantha Clark, Parvathy Menon, and more. Our most vital technologies cannot be created by those indifferent to their purpose. Today’s challenges demand builders who are guided by conviction and a clear sense of mission.

  • Learn from world-class academics, industry giants, and Palantirians.

  • Ship real projects as Deployment Strategists, Forward Deployed Engineers and Developers.

  • Study everything from Plato to software architecture.

  • Build meaningful products with the worlds most talented engineers.

  • Succeed through meritocracy and ownership.

  • Immerse yourself in reality while others prepare.
