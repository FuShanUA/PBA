Twenty years after Palantir's founding and 30 years after William Perry's Last Supper, Palantir has doubled down on its mission to grow the defense tech ecosystem.

We are equipping emerging and established Defense Industrial Base (DIB) companies with software to quickly scale mission-critical capabilities. This effort is vital to ensuring credible deterrence against our great power adversaries, and it is Palantir's contribution to the First Breakfast.
