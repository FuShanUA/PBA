About Our Partnership

The Jacobs and Palantir partnership combines Jacobs’ deep domain knowledge with Palantir’s cutting edge Data and AI technology to create value for clients. Tap into a fleet of expert data scientists, who apply their rich domain expertise, in fields like water treatment, to bring workflows to life using Palantir Foundry. The goal is to deliver real-time computing power, actionable data and control on a time horizon measured in weeks, not years.
