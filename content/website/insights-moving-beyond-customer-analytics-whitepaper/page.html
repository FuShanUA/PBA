Moving Beyond Customer Analytics

An enormous amount of customer data is being collected from different sources—from loyalty cards, to mobile app activity, to brick-and-mortar transactions. Retailers have more customer insights than ever before. But personalization at scale is very difficult to achieve with fragmented customer experiences that span various channels, products, business lines, and geographies. In this whitepaper, we explore some of the key data challenges retailers are facing today, and how Palantir Foundry is positioned to accelerate existing efforts and investments in customer data.
