Palantir Launch: Spring Program

Applications for the Spring 2025 Program are now closed!

The Palantir Launch: Spring Program is an insight program for students looking to accelerate their personal and technical growth in preparation for a successful, impactful career in tech. Across the course of one week you’ll get exposure to different aspects of Palantir’s business and the skills needed to help us solve some of the world’s hardest problems. You’ll finish the program with a deeper understanding of how a tech company works, as well as a deeper understanding of your own technical capabilities and interests.

Through interactive workshops and hands-on sessions, you’ll work collaboratively with your fellow Launchers on challenging, motivating problems; developing your skills, and broadening your perspective on the possibilities of engineering.

2025 Eligibility

The Palantir Launch: Spring Program is open to any student who meets all of the eligibility criteria below:

UK

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree at an accredited UK university and on course to graduate in 2027.

  • Currently studying Computer Science, Software Engineering or a related technical field e.g. Electrical Engineering, Physics, or Maths.

  • Applicants should be available to complete the program in-person in our London office in early April 2025 (exact dates to be confirmed). Palantir will cover travel, accommodation, and meal expenses for the duration of the program.

2025 Program Dates:

  • London, UK: April 14-17, 2025

US

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s degree at an accredited US university and on course to graduate in 2027.

  • Currently studying Computer Science, Software Engineering or a related technical field e.g. Electrical Engineering, Physics, or Maths.

  • Applicants should be available to complete the program in-person in our Washington DC or New York offices in early March 2025. Palantir will cover travel, accommodation, and meal expenses for the duration of the program.

2025 Program Dates:

  • Washington, DC: March 10-14, 2025

  • New York: March 24-28, 2025

FAQs

UK

I am a PhD student, can I apply?

No. At this time, the Palantir Launch: Spring Program is only open to Bachelor’s or Master’s students graduating in 2027.

I am enrolled in an accredited UK university, but am currently studying abroad. Can I apply?

Yes. As long as you are enrolled in an accredited UK university you are eligible to apply. Please note you must be able to complete the program in-person in London during the dates provided.

I am an international student enrolled in an accredited UK university, can I apply?

Yes.

I am enrolled on a Bachelor’s program due to finish in 2027, and then plan to complete a Master’s. Am I still eligible?

No. You must plan to complete all your studies in 2027.

Can I apply to other open Palantir opportunities?

Yes. You are welcome to apply for other open opportunities provided you meet the eligibility criteria for each one.
