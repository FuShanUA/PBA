Palantir x Snowflake

About our Partnership

Snowflake is the platform for the AI era, making it easy for enterprises to innovate faster and get more value from data. With this partnership, customers in the commercial and public sectors can leverage their data estate across Snowflake and Palantir to build more efficient and trusted data pipelines, faster data analytics, and AI applications. With the expanded integration between Foundry and Snowflake Iceberg Tables, joint customers can achieve bidirectional, zero-copy interoperability.
