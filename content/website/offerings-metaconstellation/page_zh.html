Optimize Orbital Sensors

When given a request, MetaConstellation dynamically determines which sensors are available. The constellations collaboratively schedule coverage over the area.

Sensor Integration

MetaConstellation integrates with sensor constellations, allowing users to request collection without the complexity of orchestrating many satellites.

Real-world Insights

Users request insight from MetaConstellation rather than raw data. Ask a real world question and MetaConstellation determines the optimal mix of sensors to answer that question.

03 /

Leverage AI Models

With the option of adding Edge AI to satellite sensors, Micro Models can identify objects of interest within the defined area, enabling rapid reconfiguration of the orbiting satellite based on mission need.

Tailored Algorithms

MetaConstellation leverages CI/CD AI model pipelines to find the best fit algorithm to answer user questions with every single collection.

Insights at the Edge

Users never have to start with the raw sensor data—they start with algorithmic insights from that data, whether in space or time.

Task and Track

Harness the power of the MetaConstellation by identifying and integrating with existing satellites to power more insights.

Integrate Satellites

Utilize AI-deployed sensors to task and track satellites across wide areas. As objectives evolve, feedback is received, and models are retrained.

Insights Expanded

Through tasking and tracking, MetaConstellation integrates with other satellites to collect images over areas of interest, measure changes over time, and trigger more focused or frequent imaging.

05 /

Reduce Time to Insight

In under a second, the models process imagery, detect, and geolocate objects, and determine any movement since the last collection pass, enabling rapid decision-making.

AI-enabled Sensors

Leveraging Palantir’s Edge AI technology, MetaConstellation deploys algorithms closer to the sensors—either on board satellites or at ground stations.

AI-driven Insights

Edge-deployed algorithms can identify the most relevant subsets of the sensor data, and selectively send that data to decision-makers much more rapidly.

NORTHCOM Global Information Dominance Experiment 3 (GIDE3) participants used MetaConstellation to monitor locations across the globe for indications of competitor activities.
