What is GxP

GxP is an umbrella term for “Good Practice” quality guidelines and regulations used in regulated industries, especially life sciences. The “x” denotes the specific domain and spans the product and data lifecycle to help ensure patient safety, product quality, and data integrity. Common areas include GMP (manufacturing), GCP (clinical research), GLP (nonclinical labs), GDP (distribution), and GVP (pharmacovigilance).

Support for Regulatory Compliance

Palantir Foundry’s GxP offering helps organizations meet FDA 21 CFR Part 11 and EU GMP Annex 11 expectations for electronic records, signatures, and computerized systems. Foundry GxP environments provide controls for data integrity, security, traceability, and audit trails to support validated, compliant use.
