Campaign Optimization

A large Latin American broadcast company looked to maximize returns for its advertisers and capture new value through automated campaign optimization. 

01 /

Granular, At Pace

Challenge

Typically, generating campaign proposals using a manual process took days.

The organization’s existing technology could not properly synthesize the multitude of sources needed to produce optimizations across thousands of campaigns and granular advertiser objectives. 

Solution

Today, Foundry powers a complete campaign optimization lifecycle. It formulates a “digital twin” of the ad sales business, chains together models to produce recommendations, and surfaces those recommendations to decision makers through easy-to-use applications for evaluation and action.

  • A digital twin of the ad sales business: Foundry integrates 10+ data sources — including data on subscribers, demographics, website interactions, CRM, and payments — providing visibility into inventory, reach, and demand across broadcast, cable, and digital.

  • Campaign Optimization Engine: Based on the digital twin, the customer built and operationalized new models for audience forecasts, inventory demand, dynamic pricing, and more. All of these models feed into an entirely new Optimization Engine that generates data-driven recommendations for campaigns to account executives based on advertiser goals.

  • AI/ML enabled decision making: Users input granular demographic targets and adjust reach, dispersion, and other key metrics. The Optimization Engine then examines thousands of scenarios and produces optimized campaign recommendations for the organization’s decision makers to consider. This capability has allowed account executives to proactively surface data-driven, non-obvious proposals to clients — increasing trust and satisfaction alongside ROI.
