  • 开展预测性维护

    大规模排查产品质量问题，对频发故障进行因果分析，并开展零部件测试——从而减少设备停机时间，并尽可能降低维护成本。

  • Achieve maximum efficiency with simulation models

    Integrate sensor data, inventory levels, promotion intel, and other disparate data sources. Foundry enables you to conduct refined forecasts and achieve maximum efficiency through scenario modeling.

  • Optimize working capital across production

    Identify the total value of working capital across business units, enabling identification of surplus inventory, scenario-based planning, and model deployment that improves decision making.

  • Integrate your IT and operations

    Avoid technology bloat and IT overhead. Connect your technology stack to the full operating picture. Integrate equipment, plant data, and enterprise systems to streamline processes and output.

  • Manage disruption

    Automate surfacing of supply chain disruptions to supply chain managers, including full visibility into the option space (alternative suppliers, routes, warehouses, etc.) and the possibility to simulate the consequences of the alternatives.

  • Improve yield and quality

    Connect data from the supply side of your business to analyze the raw material yield quality and pricing from different suppliers.

Airbus: Accelerated A350 airplane production by more than 30%

Foundry integrated data from schedules, crew shifts, parts, deliveries, and defects to create a single user interface to guide planning and assist trouble-shooting for every member of the A350 production team.

This allowed the team to react quickly to unforeseen changes on the production line, such as supplier delays. We expanded beyond just A350 production to create Skywise, the platform connecting the aviation industry’s value chain.
