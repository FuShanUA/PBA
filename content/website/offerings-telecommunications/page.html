Foundry for Telecommunications

Some of the world’s leading telecommunications companies use Palantir software to help drive digital transformation at scale across their business.

Foundry powers operational analytics across all facets of your CSP, enabling field engineers, quality analysts, and network operations center managers to conduct preventative maintenance, power zero-touch networks, and resolve vendor issues quickly.

Meeting the demands of telecommunications, by design

  • 01 / OPEN & INTEROPERABLE

    Foundry is open, interoperable, and modular — enabling it to seamlessly integrate with existing infrastructure and remove the risk of lock-in typically seen in traditional software products.

    Using open data formats and languages, Foundry ensures availability across the widest spectrum possible. All code is version controlled in a secure Git repository and available for external consumption and reuse.

    Foundry also has well-documented REST APIs available for interacting with the build system, managing data health, and exploring meta data, data lineage, and other information in the Foundry ecosystem from third-party applications.

    ↳ Learn More
  • 02 / ON-PREM & CLOUD-NATIVE SUPPORT

    Palantir software can be deployed either as a cloud-native SaaS offering or a fully managed on-prem solution. Both offerings provide the same world-class technology to help make your data analytics operational.

    Our Foundry platform can be utilised as a SaaS offering on top of most hyperscalers’ infrastructure: Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud (GCP), Microsoft Azure, or Oracle. With any of these data systems, Palantir leverages cloud-first technologies such as a modular, micro-service architecture, autoscaling, and distributed compute.

    For Telcos who have committed to GCP, Foundry is directly available through the Google Marketplace, so GCP customers benefit commercially as well as technically: the Foundry license and consumption can be directly offset against a customer’s existing commit.

  • 03 / DAY 1 VALUE

    From Day 1, Foundry enables telcos to deploy expert-quality applications against their most pressing challenges — from intelligent fault prediction and more accurately pinpointing the root cause of a customer problem to improving the provisioning and lead-to-cash processes.

    Foundry offers advanced patented technology and a portfolio of out-of-the-box applications specifically designed to optimize telco operations, allowing clients to customize and deploy solutions to users in days, not months.

  • 04 / BUILT FOR GDPR AND BEYOND

    In an industry with stringent regulatory and privacy constraints, telcos need powerful data protection capabilities.

    Foundry’s range of privacy and security functionalities enable telcos to keep up with evolving regulatory requirements like those mandated under GDPR and CCPA, and even go beyond what is required in order to protect the privacy and identity of individual customers.

    Beyond standard access controls, Foundry also permits users to apply dynamic data minimization procedures such as pseudonymization and encryption with purpose-based revelation to meet the complex requirements of relevant data protection regulation.

  • 05 / NO/LOW CODE FRAMEWORKS

    Foundry’s no/low-code application frameworks allow users to quickly create and adjust workflows.

    Designed for a “low floor and high ceiling” in terms of user workflow complexity, our frameworks help builders for a wide range of use cases -- from spartan applications for a small group to mission-critical applications at the heart of operations centers.

RAN Energy Savings

Reduce power usage in your network -- without impacting your customers.

ECO-RAN powered by Foundry is cloud-based, scalable SaaS that empowers mobile operators to navigate energy consumption challenges with data-driven decisions, reducing power usage and carbon emissions. ECO-RAN is delivered in partnership with 2operate, which cumulatively has more than three decades of experience building, operating, and optimizing radio access networks (RAN) at major European CSPs.

ECO-RAN Powered by Foundry

Minimize power usage up to 15% per site in your Radio Area Networks

  • Synchronize data, models, and applications to achieve operational connectivity

  • Empower network operators with foresight over your energy profile

  • Gain meaningful insight into network device performance and trigger upgrades of push configuration changes to the edge of your network

Partner
European Telecommunications Company
Summary

Within 12 weeks, ECO-RAN established that power savings of 2% - 15% per site could be delivered without impact to quality of service for mobile users.

Solution

↳ The Telco’s c5000 sites were each evaluated for power saving potential (by way of historic traffic patterns and time-specific demand)

↳ By understanding the specific utilization pattern of each site, ECO-RAN predicted a power-saving profile that delivered savings + ensured capacity was always available to match local demand

↳ ECO-RAN interacted with the site management systems to pause power saving for any sites under maintenance or upgrade to ensure network quality

↳ Required limited operator resources to initiate and operate ECO-RAN, which allowed them time to focus on core competencies

Impact

↳ Drove significant savings and immediate ROI that contributed to positive cashflow straight from initiation

↳ 5% overall power savings delivered over a one-year period

↳ 10% + anticipated avg. overall power savings, based on improved profiles and ML-based analytics

↳ Resulted in a long-term contract
