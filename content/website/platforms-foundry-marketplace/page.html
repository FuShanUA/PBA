Key Features

Publish data products in a few clicks.

Rapidly develop and deploy packages of data-backed workflows built in Foundry — your organization’s ontology, AI models, pipelines, data connections, or end-to-end use cases.

Integrated CI/CD for complex data products.

Automated version and dependency management for your complex data products, ensuring seamless synchronization of modular components as you scale across customers.

Secure collaboration among developers and consumers.

Multi-layer security allows marketplace owners to configure granular controls to enable strict governance across customer workspaces and barriers for cross-organization collaboration.

Consumer-facing storefront.

Facilitate easy discoverability for internal and external consumers to install your published data products. Recommendations allow existing consumers to extend and enhance products.

How It Works

Develop

Select a data asset or workflow built in Foundry to publish.

Deploy

Publish your data product to Marketplace with a point-and-click UI.

Discover

Consumers discover and install your data product on Marketplace.

Manage

Easily manage updates with full visibility into dependencies and automated versioning.
