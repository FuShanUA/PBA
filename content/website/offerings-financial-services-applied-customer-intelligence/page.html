A Holistic, Intelligence-Driven Approach

Built on a real-time single customer view and integrated with customer intelligence analytics, Foundry turns customer data into actionable insights.

Supercharge your CRM 

  • Banks have billions of data points available in disparate, siloed source systems. We've codified 15+ years of data integration experience into Foundry to help operationalize data at scale and pace. Using Foundry, you can build and manage your real time Customer 360, capture live interactions and decisions that power your customer engagements and unlock collaboration and exploration of your data, all with robust access controls compliant with GDPR.

Create your bank’s digital twin 

  • By connecting underlying data, analytics and models to their real-world business objects - such as Customers, Products and Channels - Foundry creates the bank’s digital twin that allows employees - from relationship managers to AML analysts - to work on a single, connected view of the customer and all their interactions with the bank. This common lexicon empowers everyone to work more efficiently, effectively & compound knowledge. 

Enable next-gen customer intelligence 

  • The bank's digital twin enables collaboration between data science and business teams on a shared foundation, making it possible to deliver AI/ML-enabled operational outcomes. Most institutions lack this common framework and develop one-off AI/ML models via point-solutions, leading to typically low return on investment. Using Foundry, you centralize model operations, deploy faster and more accurately, and ensure models integrate and evolve seamlessly with operations.

Transform customer data into insights

  • With every client interaction, Foundry automatically writes back outcomes into models, linking historically siloed, disconnected systems to power smarter, more informed decisions. This can compound value, lower costs and expand the customer intelligence layer in real-time. Foundry enables you to make decisions based on continuously evolving and relevant intelligence, and deliver a seamless, personalized customer experience that only gets better.
