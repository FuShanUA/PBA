What is Apollo?

Apollo deploys, monitors, remediates, and secures your software across heterogeneous environments.

  • It enables visibility and operational insight into your software deployment landscape within hours, while simplifying complex deployment pipelines.

Apollo Modules

  • Software Control Center
  • Autonomous Deployment — Continuous Deployment Redefined
  • Day 2 Operations

Software Control Center

Apollo gives you full visibility into - and control over - your entire deployment landscape, so you can have full confidence in your software.

A live, holistic understanding with Apollo Auto-discovery Agent

  • Gain a live picture of your current software landscape within hours, including what software and versions are installed where and liveness and readiness information.

Full visibility with Live Software Catalog

  • A live software inventory containing all available software, metadata, and the latest releases.

Deliver new features and updates at scale with Software Control Center

  • Understand exactly what is happening across all your environments with detailed health information, visibility into rollouts, and the ability to (un)install and rollback software at the click of a button.

Day 2 Operations

Maintain control with security, change management, and reliability in every phase of deployment.

Apollo helps minimize risks associated with software failures, downtime, and security vulnerabilities by identifying and addressing issues early, uniting all teams with a single view of their deployment landscape.

Day 2 Operations Capabilities:

  • Continuous vulnerability scanning

    • Identified vulnerabilities appear alongside releases so that developers know where action is needed

    • View and explore vulnerability reports

    • Configure automatic recalls

    • Suppress specific CVEs

    • Roll over strategies

  • SBOM Integration

  • Proactive monitoring and alerting

    • Define SLOs

    • Gate promotion to the next release channel based on defined SLOs and soak times

    • Automatically rollback if SLOs are violated

    • View all your observability data in one place alongside recent changes

  • Change Management

    • Enforce who can make changes and when to your environments and product configuration

    • Define intelligent approval policies
