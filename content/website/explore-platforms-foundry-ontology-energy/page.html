Your Business in Full Fidelity.

Foundry’s Ontology transforms your digital assets — including data, models, and processes — into a dynamic, actionable representation of the business for all users to leverage in operations:

  • Objects digitally represent the real-world entities (ex. rigs, compressors), relationships, and events that constitute your business.

  • Relations represent the connections between real world entities, events and processes.

  • Actions capture the kinetics between objects and orchestrate real world change through your enterprise systems. Actions can be mapped from existing processes or existing models.

Hydrate Your Ontology.

Synchronize data, models, and applications to achieve operational connectivity — in days, not years.

Data Hydration

  • Operationalize data at scale and pace with in-platform capabilities like HyperAuto and Pipeline Builder.

  • Utilize 200+ out of the box connectors and native federation capabilities to seamlessly synchronize multimodal data into your Ontology from across the enterprise: structured, unstructured, streaming, IoT, transactional, geospatial data, and more. 

  • Built-in role-, classification-, and purpose-based access controls let you institute granular privacy and security controls.

Model Hydration

  • Foundry binds your models to the Ontology, allowing them to be governed, released, and injected directly into analytics and applications.

  • Deploy your existing model assets at scale to drive operations. Through Actions, model outputs can be expressed not just as insights but as actions that users can take — closing the loop between AI/ML and operations.

  • Manage your models in production applications with Model Objectives. Objectives specify a challenge and record context, relevant data sources, metadata, and logic — aligning model development and evaluation with operational goals.

Wield Your Ontology.

Access the power of object-aware analytics and applications out of the box — or build for a rapidly changing future.

Ontology APIs

Leverage a full range of APIs that read from and enrich the Ontology to build your own custom apps. The Ontology can also fuel third-party BI tools.

Compose object-aware applications in-platform using Slate and Workshop — low-code workflow builders that leverage the full read-write power of the Ontology. Automatic API generation for all elements in the Ontology mean that you can also build applications in the environment of your choice.
