About the Conference Series

The Atlantic & Pacific Forum is The Palantir Foundation's signature conference series, bringing together key thought leaders, practitioners, and policy makers from across government, industry, and academia.

Our forum addresses the most pressing challenges in defense, artificial intelligence, and international security, fostering critical dialogue on how emerging technologies are reshaping the global security landscape.
