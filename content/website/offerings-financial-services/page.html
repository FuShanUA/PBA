Partner
Global Investment Bank
Challenge

A global bank struggled to understand its client base across multiple jurisdictions. As a result, Know Your Customer (KYC) and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) operations were inefficient, and the bank faced severe tax, AML, and risk exposure.

Solution

Machine learning models automatically resolve billions of records from around the world into a Single Client View (SCV) - bank employees now have a single point of access to search the full scope of clean customer data while retaining secure access controls.

Impact

  • 15,000 global users
  • 2PB data scale
  • 100+ use cases across the enterprise

Challenge

Teams hesitated to experiment with new campaigns because manipulating data was prone to error, defining cohorts took weeks or months, and pushing new models to production could take up to 6 months.

Solution
