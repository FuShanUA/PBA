Challenge

Fujitsu is a leading Information and Communication Technology (ICT) company in Japan, offering a full range of technology products, solutions and services. Approximately 130,000 Fujitsu staff support customers in more than 100 countries.

In order to deliver the best-possible service to this large, globalized customer base, Fujitsu engaged Palantir to support the modernization of its technological infrastructure and the optimization of its supply chain.

Solution

Palantir Foundry was deployed at Fujitsu in order to integrate over 40 data systems, connecting various ERP software systems with other critical systems of record. Thanks to software-defined data integration, this harmonization was completed in days, not years, creating immediate value for Fujitsu.

The result of this best-in-class data integration effort is a common operating picture whose front-end capabilities power a diverse range of workflows - from resource allocation, to financial planning, to supply chain optimization, across Japan and internationally. Every Fujitsu user, including Operations Managers, Financial Analysts, Service Specialists, Engineers and Executives, can tap into the power of the organization’s data, accelerating decision-making and improving customer service.

Palantir CEO Alex Karp comments: “The partnership with Fujitsu further reflects our commitment to Japan — to ensuring that the country’s leading commercial institutions and government agencies have the software they need to do their jobs, whether it is responding to a public health crisis or defending the nation.”
