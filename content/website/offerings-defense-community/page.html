PalVets

Representing Palantir’s diverse military Veteran community.

The mission of PalVets is to support and grow our sense of belonging, and to provide an inclusive venue to share experiences and support each other as we continue to serve our community in a different capacity.

We learn from each other and help educate the broader Palantir community about Vets, and engage and support external parties that address current Veteran issues.

Community Partnerships

Championing our Armed Forces.

America's Game

Palantir is the Official Software sponsor of the U.S. Army-Navy Football Game, presented by USAA. We are honored not only to be able to support the work of the U.S. military but are excited to share in its traditions as well. 

Supporting military personnel, both past and present. 

Leashes of Valor

Palantir supports Leashes of Valor, a national nonprofit that provides highly-trained service dogs to post-9/11 Veterans to help them adjust, acclimate, and thrive in their lives after military service.
