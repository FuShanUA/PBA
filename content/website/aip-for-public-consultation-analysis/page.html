AIP for Public Consultation Analysis

AIP for Public Consultation Analysis elevates the analysis of public consultation feedback, aggregating large datasets of responses and empowering subject matter experts to swiftly identify critical issues and gauge the sentiment of solicited responses for major program initiatives.

Planning and permitting processes for public infrastructure projects often elicit thousands of public comments, all of which require thorough analysis as part of conscientious project execution. AIP adeptly consolidates feedback from both structured and unstructured channels, such as forms and emails, providing relevant summaries of the consolidated feedback. By learning from the expertise of Subject Matter Experts (SMEs), AIP enhances the visibility of feedback topics and sentiment, ensuring that every voice is heard and considered in the decision-making process. Key Features:

  • Diverse Feedback Ingestion to meet the public where they are: AIP captures and processes public comments from a multitude of channels, including email and web forms meeting the public in their preferred communication methods.

  • Expert in the Loop: SMEs contribute their knowledge to validate and refine the insights generated by AIP, continually enhancing the model's accuracy and relevance while grounding those insights in the original response text.

  • Free-form Analysis: Utilizing the Ontology, project planners can delve into the feedback, connecting comments by topic for a deeper, more organized analysis.

  • Uncovering New Insights: AIP's advanced models are adept at detecting underlying patterns within the comments, revealing insights that may otherwise go unnoticed.

AIP for Public Consultation Analysis is an invaluable tool for project teams, offering a sophisticated approach to understanding and integrating public feedback, thereby ensuring that public works projects are informed by the diverse perspectives of the communities they serve.
