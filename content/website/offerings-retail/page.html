About Us

Palantir was founded in 2003 and builds data-driven operations and decision-making software to empower some of the world's most important institutions.

We got our start in intelligence and defense, then applied our frontline deployment learnings to support numerous industries and commercial enterprises across the globe.

Our core commercial platform Palantir Foundry is the AI-powered operating system for the modern enterprise. Foundry supercharges your existing technology investments to bridge siloed planning, optimise for efficiency and agility, and effectively forecast.

Palantir for Retail

In order to navigate today’s shocks to supply and demand, retailers need near-instant visibility into demand and the ability to adapt their promotions, inventory, and operations in real time.

Foundry enables retailers to build a unified data foundation and solve some of their most complex problems — from optimising inventory levels, to building better supply chains, to delivering omnichannel customer experiences, and more.

Top global enterprise retailers are using Foundry to drive digital transformation and make better, smarter use of their data.

Partner
Global Industrial Manufacturer
Challenge

The customer was wasting significant amounts of expired material, costing tens of millions of dollars per year. They needed to analyse data across a vast supply chain network of hundreds of plants with more than 100,000 products to deliberate inventory reduction.

Solution

With Palantir Foundry, they integrated production, equipment capacity, plant assets, and logistics data into a single data layer, providing visibility across all products and plants. The customer was able to perform granular inventory monitoring and track intervention effectiveness so they could alter scrap reduction strategies -- all based on workflows created and performed in Foundry.

Impact

↳ $50M+ of inventory management optimisation savings identified within weeks

↳ 100,000 products covered to optimise inventory levels in global network

Partner
Large European Retail Group
Challenge

The customer was experiencing out-of-stock- and overstock issues, especially around their promotions. The result was eroding margins and loss of revenue.

Solution

With Palantir Foundry, their entire supply chain was virtualized, delivering full visibility into decision-making across operations and powering both granular forecasting models and no-code product promotion workflows. The customer also used a transparent, rules-based forecast model to analyze historical data -- including out-of-stock, seasonality, and changes in sales -- in order to predict optimal quantities of promotion items.

Impact

↳ 20% decrease in out-of-stock goods within 3 months

↳ 25% decrease in out-of-stock incidents and waste

↳ 20 TB unstructured data integrated

Partner
Large Beverage Company
Challenge

The customer wanted to optimise and manage their $10bn+ of annual revenue by creating an integrated, end-to-end sales and revenue management system.

Solution

Foundry streamlined the company’s repricing workflow with clustering methodology, creating a more standardized and contextually informed annual price increase process across 1M+ products. Localized elasticity models surfaced revenue tradeoffs between opposing priorities such as increasing prices and increasing volume.

Impact

↳ $5M saved from flagging & addressing bad product mix within wholesalers

↳ $2.5M saved by identifying where wholesalers were misaligned with strategy

↳ $3M saved on new discount splits with wholesalers

↳ 2.2X increase in SKU price adjustments following pricing optimization workflow
