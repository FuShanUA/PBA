Powering Innovation for Doosan Infracore

Since 2019, Palantir has partnered with Doosan Infracore to drive the heavy machinery manufacturer’s digital transformation and deliver high-quality, market-relevant products to its customers.

Play Video

By creating the data foundation “DI360,” powered by Palantir Foundry, Doosan has begun to connect their company and revolutionize workflows across the business. Key workflows include:

New Product Development:

  • Accelerate R&D by monitoring key development targets like sales volume, price, profitability, quality levels and fluctuations in market share, in real-time.

  • Compare new option trends across countries, models, and time to increase market relevancy of new products.

Supply Chain Management:

  • Monitor the recovery speed of market demand in each country through new market signal detection methods, including ways to leverage high-scale edge data from TMS (Telematics System).

  • Develop dynamic and adaptive demand forecasts that can keep pace with the market as it changes due to the global pandemic, trade regionalization, and other global trends.

  • Detect order risk and proactively manage to reduce risk in supply chain execution.

Aftermarket & Product Support:

  • Analyze field claims to detect and root cause issues across the fleet.

  • Plan and manage aftermarket sales and part inventory to continue to deliver high quality service to customers throughout the lifetime of their machines.

Beyond improving individual functions, DI360 allows separate functions to share data seamlessly and effectively. Quality information coming from warranty claims can be fed back to the R&D team to improve models into the future. Market trends detected by the Supply Chain team can also inform new research about where the market is heading. DI360 connects diverse functions in a virtuous cycle.

In this way, Doosan Infracore is “powered by innovation,” both in the high-quality machines they produce and in how they use of technology to run an effective, resilient business.
