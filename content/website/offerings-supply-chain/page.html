About Our Work

From humanitarian aid to vital manufacturing components, Palantir’s software is the backbone of critical supply chains across the globe. Foundry — our platform for AI-powered operations — supercharges your existing technology investments to bridge siloed planning and execution processes, optimize inventory management, and help build supply chain resilience for economic and geopolitical uncertainty.
