Granular Lineage-aware Deletion in Palantir Foundry

We make data deletion safe and reliable for our government and commercial partners with state-of-the-art data lineage tracking, regardless of scale or complexity.

Foundry comes out of the box with data lineage tracking capabilities that account for every transformation of a piece of data. This means that, no matter what happens to a piece of data or where it ends up in the platform, Foundry can help institutions comprehensively perform deletions throughout the system.

Our lineage-aware data deletion capabilities help organizations meet compliance obligations in evolving regulatory environments.

Organizations use Palantir Foundry as their secure and accountable infrastructure to maximize the utility of their data while making sure that it's processed in accordance with the rules, regulations, and norms that govern data privacy across industries.
