Stability, Visibility, Reliability

In telecommunications, complete visibility into network assets, the customers they serve, and technical issues that arise present a complex data challenge: it is difficult to determine root causes and the globally optimal actions to take.

Solution

A major European telecommunications company uses Foundry to build a complete digital twin of their physical network and link it to customer information. 

This has allowed analysts and engineers to better triage and resolve customer-impacting incidents, proactively replace faulty equipment, manage their field force and optimize their provisioning process. Today, previously-siloed teams are able to work on a unified ontology, powering mission-critical use cases across the enterprise, and helping reduce costs and ultimately increase customer satisfaction.

Impact

  • 20% year-over-year reduction in customer complaints — exceeding targets by 200% — driven significantly by Foundry

  • 3-week average lead time for business users to develop a new use case
