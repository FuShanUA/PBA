Translate your real-world complexity into real-time adaptation. All while planning for the future.

Key Features

Point-and-click problem formulation.

Encode your operational constraints and define permitted and optimal behavior in schedules with a point-and-click, low-code interface using Foundry Rules. Configure temporal and condition-based logic in the Foundry Ontology.

Full impact visibility and response.

Surface conflicts and dependencies from all actions (actual and proposed), enabling users to evaluate the impact(s) to cross-business processes before implementation and to determine viable alternatives during disruptions.

Condition-based triggers.

Configure complex conditional logic to automatically trigger actions in response to schedule changes, permeating downstream impacts across disparate systems and teams.

Collaboration on a common planning interface.

Enable a variety of user profiles — from operators to data scientists — to interact with all facets of scheduling with customizable UIs and interoperability with external platforms.

Cross-time horizon planning.

Resolve conflicts between short and long-term plans to meet business KPIs using a drag and drop-style Gantt chart with a built-in simulator.

Optimization.

Integrate with external third-party solvers using Foundry APIs or build custom recommendation engines for dynamic plans in the long term and to identify incremental opportunities in real-time.

How It Works

Connect.

Establish a real-time common operating picture for your entire organization with the Foundry Ontology, integrating all relevant data and models with out-of-the-box connectors. 

Define.

Encode constraints, chain dependencies, and define ideal behavior. 

Optimize.

Surface limitations and optimality of actions to teams throughout your business. 

Learn.

Visualize and interrogate interventions to long-term plans and evaluate past decisions to plan for the future and build resiliency.
