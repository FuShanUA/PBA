01 Apollo：随处部署

扩展实例而无需扩展 DevOps

Apollo 在异构环境中部署、监控、修复并保障您的软件安全。它能在数小时内实现对软件部署环境的可见性和运营洞察，同时简化复杂的部署管道。

  • 数小时内洞悉您的软件部署环境 → 自动发现与软件控制中心

    • Apollo 让您对整个部署环境拥有全面的可见性和控制力，从而对您的软件充满信心。

  • 可靠地部署软件并管理版本发布 → 持续部署

    • 快速、可靠、轻松地部署新功能，将时间投入到创新中，而非编排上。

  • 高效支持您的软件 → 持续部署 + 二次运维

    • 在部署的每个阶段保持对安全、变更管理和可靠性的控制。

  • 将安全设为默认选项以巩固合规性 → 安全运维

    • 通过集成的安全运维增强信心：持续漏洞扫描、可操作的扫描结果、自动召回/回滚，以及自动执行的安全策略。

02 Ontology SDK

加速数据访问

  • 极速开发应用

    • 只需极少的代码即可访问本体，快速开发应用程序。

    • 更好的开发者体验意味着更好的应用，以及与运营用户之间更快的反馈闭环。

  • Never locked-in

    • OSDK reduces switching cost at the data layer and the application layer - critical for true interoperability.

    • New interoperable applications can be developed and deployed through the Ontology API, and the switching cost away from Palantir is bound to other vendors pulling data into their platforms via APIs.

  • A Trusted Partner

    • For government agencies and qualified companies, we can provide a low-side environment to develop against actual ontologies for targeted use cases with notional data.

03 FedStart

Eliminate the Accreditation Barrier to Deploy Easily.

FedStart supports companies to be FedRAMP and IL-compliant, rapidly enabling accreditation in weeks, at a fraction of the cost of traditional paths.

How it works

  • Enables innovators to run their products within Palantir’s secure, already-accredited environment in three simple steps:

    • Containerize your application and create a Helm chart

    • Deploy your application using Apollo into the FedStart environment

    • Complete security integration

Why Fedstart?

  • Seamless Integration

  • Usage-based Pricing

  • FedRAMP and IL5 compliance managed by Palantir.

  • Government ATO conversations, compliance artifacts, continuous monitoring, and control assessments managed by Palantir.
