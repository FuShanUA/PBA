What is PGWS?
Palantir Government Web Services (PGWS) encompasses three initial offerings - Apollo, FedStart, and the Ontology SDK - that enable defense tech innovators to leverage our hard-earned experience and get from 0-1 faster.
Who is PGWS for?
PGWS serves the entire defense tech ecosystem, and provides developers with the tools they need to build, ship and manage the innovative solutions of tomorrow, today.
How do I get started?
Whether you're already on your journey to bring your solutions to government or you're just getting started, we're here to help you fast track your journey to the frontline.

01 Apollo: Deploy Anywhere

Scale instances without scaling DevOps

Apollo deploys, monitors, remediates, and secures your software across heterogeneous environments. It enables visibility and operational insight into your software deployment landscape within hours, while simplifying complex deployment pipelines.

  • Understand your software deployment landscape within hours → Auto-discovery & Software Control Center

    • Apollo gives you full visibility into - and control over - your entire deployment landscape, so you can have full confidence in your software.

  • Reliably deploy your software and manage releases → Continuous Deployment

    • Deploy new features quickly, reliably, and with ease so you can spend time innovating instead of orchestrating.

  • Efficiently support your software → CD + Day 2 Ops

    • Maintain control with security, change management, and reliability in every phase of deployment.

  • Fortify compliance by making security the default → SecOps

    • Gain confidence with integrated SecOps: Continuous vulnerability scanning, actionable results, automatic recalls / rollbacks, as well as auto-enforced security policies

03 FedStart

Eliminate the Accreditation Barrier to Deploy Easily.

FedStart supports companies to be FedRAMP and IL-compliant, rapidly enabling accreditation in weeks, at a fraction of the cost of traditional paths.

How it works

  • Enables innovators to run their products within Palantir’s secure, already-accredited environment in three simple steps:

    • Containerize your application and create a Helm chart

    • Deploy your application using Apollo into the FedStart environment

    • Complete security integration

Why Fedstart?

  • Seamless Integration

  • Usage-based Pricing

  • FedRAMP and IL5 compliance managed by Palantir.

  • Government ATO conversations, compliance artifacts, continuous monitoring, and control assessments managed by Palantir.
