Palantir and Data Protection

A comprehensive approach to privacy and governance

Around the world, Palantir is helping customers meet their compliance obligations in ever-evolving regulatory environments.

At Palantir, we're committed to helping organizations protect privacy and strengthen trust in how they use information and achieve the potential of their digital transformation.

Organizations use Palantir Foundry as their secure and accountable infrastructure to maximize the utility of their data while making sure that it's processed in accordance with the rules, regulations, and norms that govern data privacy.

Among others, Palantir currently offers the following data protection modules, which were specifically developed to help our customers comply with data subject rights requests under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and comparable privacy laws in other jurisdictions (e.g., Brazil’s Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados Pessoais (LGPD), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and its recent amendments through the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), etc.

Data Subject Access Request Module

This module seeks to assist organizations with implementing Article 15 GDPR or comparable privacy regulations, giving data subjects the right to obtain confirmation from the controller whether any data is being processed about them as well as access to basic information about that data.

Right to be Forgotten Module

This module seeks to assist organizations with Article 17 GDPR or comparable privacy regulations, giving data subjects the right to request erasure of data being processed about them, colloquially known as the “Right to be Forgotten” or “Right to Erasure.” 

Both of these modules can be deployed together as they share a common data asset that tracks data sources and sensitive data.
