Revolutionizing Renewables

A wind operator had limited visibility into systematic issues and maintenance needs across its fleet of turbines. Data was distributed in multiple different systems, and in inaccessible formats by third-parties such as PDF, making analysis difficult.

Solution

Data Transparency

Foundry was used to make data about turbine attributes, components, maintenance and inspection accessible to the business, creating a common understanding of maintenance performance. Foundry parsed internal and external PDF inspection reports to unlock data for fleet wide analysis. LIDAR and other sensor data was integrated to track wind, energy and financial profiles.

Analytics for Operations

Foundry established a data foundation that enables KPI generation, benchmarking of asset performance, and in depth analysis. Foundry delivered detailed views on maintenance costs per turbines, both from loss of income and servicing. Probabilities of failure and co-failures for turbine parts were made centrally available from alarm codes and service reports across the fleet.

Business Enablement

Foundry allowed business users to be self-sufficient, creating their own no-code analyses. Technical users are now driving further value creation by enriching and extending the data asset.

Use Cases

  • Identification of fleet wide maintenance issues to be fixed by the manufacturer under warranty

  • Detailed OpEx planning and forecasting across turbine types in the portfolio

  • Automated maintenance planning; integrating processing of people, resources, parts

  • Optimization of downtime windows for maintenance
