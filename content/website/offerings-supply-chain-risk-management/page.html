Across Continents and Cyberspace

Government supply chains are among the most complex, consequential systems on Earth - supporting both national security and public well-being. 

However, with supply chains stretching across continents and cyberspace, threats can emerge any time, anywhere. For instance, the COVID-19 pandemic and SolarWinds cyber attack revealed critical vulnerabilities, jeopardizing government information and assets.

Effective risk management and resilience requires the ability to monitor a dynamic digital twin of the entire supply chain. Palantir Foundry helps protect agencies against catastrophic supply chain disruptions by integrating siloed, cross-domain data into a secure, collaborative environment. Moreover, Foundry’s AI-agnostic platform enables institutions to deploy best-in-class commercial or bespoke risk models to rapidly surface and mitigate threats before they compound.
